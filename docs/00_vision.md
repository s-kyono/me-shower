# Vision

me-shower is not just a resume generator.

It is a Personal Career Operating System for growing **Career Knowledge** from daily work traces, evidence, judgment, learning, review, and reflection.

> Career Knowledge is the core.

The center of me-shower is not Resume, PDF, Skills, Timeline, Agents, or generated artifacts. The center is Career Knowledge: structured knowledge that preserves what a person did, why they did it, how they judged it, what they learned, and which evidence supports it.

> Resume is an output, not the source of truth.

Resume is an important deliverable, but it is not the canonical source. PDF, Timeline, Agents, and generated artifacts are support layers or views for growing, validating, and presenting Career Knowledge.

> A career is not written once. It grows through evidence, review, and reflection.

A career should not be reconstructed only when someone applies for a role. It should be grown continuously from actual work, supporting evidence, human judgment, and repeated reflection.

## What me-shower Optimizes For

me-shower does not optimize for producing polished resume claims as quickly as possible.

It optimizes for building durable Career Knowledge from real work traces:

- Work leaves traces in GitHub, Slack, Teams, Daily Reports, and local memos
- Those traces become evidence candidates
- Evidence is normalized into reviewable Canonical Events
- Reviewed events strengthen Career Knowledge
- Views such as Resume or PDF are generated only when needed

This ordering matters. It allows audience-specific outputs to change without distorting the underlying knowledge.

## Why Resume Is Not the Center

Resume is audience-specific and context-specific. It changes depending on the role, company, and story being emphasized.

Career Knowledge is more durable. It sits above any single output format and can later be rendered into multiple views, such as:

- Resume
- PDF
- GitHub Profile
- Portfolio
- Interview Stories
- Proposal Drafts
- Skill Inventory
- Weekly Career Review
- Source Timeline

me-shower is therefore not a tool for producing one good-looking resume. It is a system for growing the knowledge base that can support many outputs.

## The Role of AI and Human Review

AI helps read sources, structure candidate events, propose interpretations, and generate views.

AI must not become the long-term source of truth.

> AI proposes. Human reviews. Career Knowledge persists.

Human Review exists to stop exaggeration, leakage, incorrect extraction, and unsupported claims before they become durable knowledge.

## Non-Negotiables

These are non-negotiable constraints. If an implementation, workflow, or PR violates them, that change should be treated as suspect even if it appears convenient.

- me-shower exists to grow Career Knowledge, not to decorate Resume output
- It must not create claims before evidence
- AI proposals must not become persistent Career Knowledge without Human Review
- Skills are operational knowledge for agents, not the asset being grown
- Timeline is a view for inspecting flow, not history itself
- Raw source is input material, not long-term truth
- Generated artifacts are regenerable outputs, not sources of truth
- Confidential information must not be preserved as Career Knowledge
- Convenience must not distort Career Knowledge
- Resume polish must not overstate the person’s actual work or judgment

## v0.x and v1.0.0

v0.x is for learning, validation, and boundary discovery.

At this stage, the project may temporarily tolerate a large `main.py` or implementation density if that helps validate Source Intelligence, Evidence Guard, Confidence, Timeline, and review boundaries.

v1.0.0 is for architecture consolidation. That consolidation must not weaken the core principles:

- Career Knowledge remains the center
- Evidence remains prior to claims
- Views remain downstream outputs
- Human Review remains a prerequisite for persistent knowledge
